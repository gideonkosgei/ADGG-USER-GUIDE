PARASITE INFECTION

Access the parasite infection Screen by clicking the parasite infection tab on the animal health screen.
A grid will be displayed that list previous parasite infection records for the animal.



Add New Record

To add a new parasite infection event record, click on the add new icon on the parasite infection event listing grid toolbox.



Capture the details of the new parasite infection event and save.
The new record will be listed on the parasite infection events grid.
