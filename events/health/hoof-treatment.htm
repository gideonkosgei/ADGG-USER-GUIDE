Hoof Treatment

Access the hoof treatment Screen by clicking the hoof treatment tab on the animal health screen.
A grid will be displayed that list previous hoof treatment records for the animal.



Add New Record

To add a new hoof health event record, click on the add new icon on the hoof treatment event listing grid toolbox.



Capture the details of the new hoof treatment event and save.
The new record will be listed on the hoof treatment events grid.
