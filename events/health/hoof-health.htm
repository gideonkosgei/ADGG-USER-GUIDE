Hoof health

Access the hoof health Screen by clicking the hoof health tab on the animal health screen.
A grid will be displayed that list previous hoof health records for the animal.



Add New Record

To add a new hoof health event record, click on the add new icon on the hoof health event listing grid toolbox.



Capture the details of the new hoof health event and save.
The new record will be listed on the hoof health events grid.
