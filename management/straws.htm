All AI straws need to be registered first before they can be used for insemination.
Straws that are not in use but already registered need to be retired.

Menu Location

Management> AI straws


The landing page of the AI straw menu displays a list of all registered and active straws
Once a straw is registered, it will be available on the AI event as a drop-down option.
